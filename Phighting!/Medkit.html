Medkit is a very useful support character in the game, as he is the only character in the entire game that can heal. Playing Medkit boils down to keeping your teammates alive, and also keeping yourself alive. Medkit's teleport is useful for mobility, but also for healing. Medkit should not be used for mainly combat, but if you're good, you can take on a squad of enemies and maybe get a kill. However, Medkit's main end -of-game stat is assists, as you can get an absurd amount of assists (I got 30 once!). This is because the game counts your healing as eligible for assists, too. Medkit is a very fun character to play if you're good at it.

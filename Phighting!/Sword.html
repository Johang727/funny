Sword is a melee character who is pretty beginner-friendly, as his abilities give him good mobility and damage output, along with ease of use. He has an high amount of hitpoints, and as such is one of the easiest phighters to heal. His phinisher is a very good crowd control method, as it does AoE damage and knocks back enemies, perfect for the Capture game mode.

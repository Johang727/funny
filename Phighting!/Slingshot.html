Slingshot is a middle difficulty Phighter to play. On one hand, one needs to be able to aim good while holding down a mouse button. On the other, the many attacks that Slingshot has that have spread are good for people who don't have good aim. His Phinisher can pass through walls, which is good for crowd control behind cover.

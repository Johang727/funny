Boombox plays very different to any other character in Phighting. His main attack revolves around a little rhythm game, and you have to maintain a streak in it to start doing large amounts of damage. His Phinisher is one of the best for support, as it gives an outrageous amount of shields. Note: Some of Boombox's attacks are dependent on number of charges. Boombox's charges rise with his streak, with a max. of 4 charges.
